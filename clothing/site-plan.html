Overview

Purpose

Are you wearing clothes that are durable and made of good quality? Do you want the best out of your purchase and get your money’s worth? I want to revolutionize the clothing game and build a clothing website called M & B Clothing. We will have state-of-the-art clothes showcased on a website that has a catalog for both men and women, with competitor standards, good quality, and at an affordable price. I want to make something that everyone can wear and show off to the world. I want my website to be presentable to our viewers and also the products we publish to the public.

Audience

We want to seek everyone’s style of appeal and we want to bring the best of our products. We want to build the user experience through having a good visual website and being able to present state-of-the-art clothing. As we bring world-class products, we must show them a catalog and a variety of product that represents our brand. Innovating and changing the game on how our users see our clothes will revolutionize the fashion industry.
